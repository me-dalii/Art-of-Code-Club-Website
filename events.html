Opening/Hackathon 2.0

First of many events in the 2022/2023 academic year:
The Opening/Hackathon took place at the Faculty of Sciences of Bizerte, a two-in-one event to start a new adventure with the AOC Club. A new journey full of challenges and ambitions in an effort to learn, share and connect.

  • OPENING: The opening began with a presentation and a brief overview of our working environment, our structure and our new executive board. With our previous and upcoming projects and visions demonstrating why AOC is your favorable choice to improve both your soft and hard skills while boosting your future career!
  • Hackathon: The main goal of our Hackathon is to bring innovative minds with an appetite for knowledge to collaborate and challenge in order to succeed and grow 🚀 Over 24 hours, 20 teams of 3 people were tasked with developing a web OR mobile application solution for a "Social Crises" topic.
Special thanks to all the FSB, ISET Bizerte, ISET Zaghouan, INSAT, ISI, FST and IPEIB teams who participated in this edition and we congratulate the INSAT "0x45" team for winning first place.
Thank you to everyone who was there from our dear members who worked very hard to make this happen, Mr. Habib Fathallah, Administration, to our technical support and friends.
Many thanks to our 3 judges Dr Fadi Kacem, Mr Anas Tradi and Mohamed Barkaoui for accepting the invitation.
This event is in partnership with 4C-FS Bizerte and sponsored by STB Bank الشركة التونسية للبنك

Spring Boot Workshop

Spring is the most widely used framework in the industry today. Looking at any java developer job posting you will see Spring Boot. This Workshop is designed for experienced as well as complete beginners to learn the basics of Spring Boot in easy steps. This workshop offers hands-on experience building Spring Framework applications using Spring Boot.

UI/UX Workshop

Adobe Xd is a powerful tool for UI/UX designers giving you the power to design and prototype an entire app, all from one program. This course starts off with Adobe Xd basics and quickly gets up to speed on designing and prototyping a full-fledged app with amazing images, colors, and animations. This course is aimed at people interested in UI/UX Design. We’ll start from the very beginning and work all the way through, step by step. If you already have some UI/UX Design experience but want to get up to speed using Adobe XD then this course is perfect for you too! You’ll also receive direct support and insightful guidance from the workshop instructor and technical support, so you’ll never be far from a helping hand.
