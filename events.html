Opening / Hackathon Event.

Opening day and hackathon to start up the journey. a lot of effort has been made to make this happen.

The Opening started with a presentation and a brief sneak peek on our work environment, structure and executive board. Along with our previous and upcoming projects and visions demonstrating why AOC is your favorable choice to improve both your soft and hard skills along with boosting your future career! The Hackathon main purpose is to bring innovative minds with an appetite for knowledge to collaborate and challenge in order to succeed and grow. Over 24-hours, Every team has been tasked with developing a web application solution for our topic. A lot of teams has done an amazing job on completing the time and made it to the end.
